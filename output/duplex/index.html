Duplex

A duplex is a residential building divided into two separate living units.

Description

In the Indian real estate market, a duplex refers to a type of housing that consists of two distinct living spaces, usually situated within the same structure. Each unit typically has its own entrance, kitchen, and bathroom facilities, allowing for privacy and independence. Duplexes can be found in various configurations, such as side-by-side units or one unit on top of the other, often within a single plot of land. They are popular among families looking for a spacious living environment, as well as investors interested in rental opportunities. Duplexes can be designed as single-family homes or multi-family residences, catering to different market segments. In urban areas, duplexes are often seen as a practical solution to housing shortages, providing more living space in a compact format. Additionally, they can be an attractive option for extended families, allowing multiple generations to live together while maintaining their own personal space.

Examples

  • A two-story duplex with separate entrances for each unit.
  • A side-by-side duplex where two families share a common wall.

Additional Information

  • Duplexes are often more affordable than single-family homes in urban areas.
  • They can provide rental income potential if one unit is rented out.
