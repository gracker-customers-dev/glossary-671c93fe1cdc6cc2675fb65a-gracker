Apartment

A self-contained residential unit within a building, typically part of a larger complex.

Description

In the Indian real estate market, an apartment refers to a residential unit that is part of a larger building or complex. These units are designed for individual living and often come with shared amenities such as parking, gardens, gyms, and security services. Apartments can vary in size, ranging from studio apartments, which are compact and efficient living spaces, to larger multi-bedroom units suitable for families. They can be found in various configurations, including high-rise buildings, mid-rise complexes, and low-rise structures. Apartments are popular among urban dwellers due to their convenience, proximity to workplaces, and availability of essential services. Additionally, the growth of urbanization in India has led to increased demand for apartments, making them a significant segment in the real estate market. Buyers may choose to purchase apartments for personal use or as investment properties, with the potential for rental income. Various types of apartments include luxury apartments, affordable housing units, and serviced apartments, catering to different demographics and income levels.

  • Many apartments are governed by housing societies that maintain common areas and facilities.
