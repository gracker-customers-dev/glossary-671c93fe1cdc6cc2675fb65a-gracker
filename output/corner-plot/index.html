Corner plot

A corner plot is a piece of land that is located at the intersection of two or more roads.

Description

In the context of the Indian real estate market, a corner plot is highly sought after due to its unique positioning. These plots are typically situated at the junction of two streets, allowing for greater visibility and accessibility. This prime location is advantageous for both residential and commercial purposes. Corner plots often benefit from the potential for better natural lighting and ventilation, as they tend to have more open sides compared to interior plots. Additionally, properties on corner plots often attract a premium price due to their desirability and the convenience they offer. For businesses, a corner plot can mean increased foot traffic and visibility, while for homeowners, it can provide the advantages of space and privacy. However, potential buyers should also consider factors such as traffic noise and safety, particularly if the plot is located on a busy intersection. Overall, corner plots hold significant value in the Indian real estate landscape.

Examples

  • A residential corner plot in a gated community that offers improved access and views.
  • A commercial corner plot on a busy street, ideal for retail establishments.

Additional Information

  • Corner plots often have zoning advantages, allowing for wider construction options.
  • They may come with higher property taxes due to their prime location.
